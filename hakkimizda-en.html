About Us

Our History

2011

Establishment of Target Communication

Target Communication set out with the goal of becoming a pioneer in the call center industry. With its expert staff, it quickly gained recognition in the sector by advancing with the aim of creating competitive advantage and added value.

2015

Rapid Growth in the Industry

By gradually increasing the number of personnel, our company became one of the leading organizations in the sector. Our customer portfolio expanded to include important brands from different industries.

2020

Technology Vision

Our company, closely following technological developments, initiated R&D activities to leverage AI solutions for enhancing call center operations.

2023

Establishment of Target Tech

Target Technology was established with the aim of combining the world of Artificial Intelligence with the call center industry. Our company aims to pioneer digital transformation in the field of customer service with innovative artificial intelligence solutions.

2024

First Prototype

We developed the prototype of our first AI-based customer representative software and achieved successful test results. We are preparing to raise industry standards by optimizing this technology.

2025

Coming Soon...

We aim to introduce our AI-based customer representative to the service of the industry. This technology will be the beginning of a new era in the field of customer service.

Our Mission

As Target Tech, our mission is to optimize call center operations, increase efficiency, and elevate customer experience to the highest level by using artificial intelligence technologies. With the solutions we offer, we aim to enable companies to use their human resources most efficiently while not compromising on customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in artificial intelligence integration in the call center industry and a the pioneer of digital transformation in the field of customer service. With the technologies we develop, we aim to redefine the industry standard by providing data-driven and personalized customer experiences.
